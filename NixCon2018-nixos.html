Origin Story

  • 2016-02: Old wiki in read-only mode
  • 2017-02: Some discussion (97 comments)
    “Can we undeprecate/unlock the wiki?”
  • 2017-04: Creation of nixos-users wiki on GitHub
  • 2017-05: Link to old wiki removed
  • 2017-06: Creation of nixos.wiki

Details at https://nixos.wiki/wiki/NixOS_Wiki:History
